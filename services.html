What we offer

Basic

A101 Basic Archery focuses on beginning archery safety, skills, and drills. Participants learn archerfy form, range rules, and etiquette through hands-on training and fun games.

Equipment provided with exception of safety kit.

Cost: $120/month

Pre-reqs:

  • Interest in learning about the sport of archery
  • 8 years and older (7 year olds with pre-evaluation will be considered)

Intermediate

A102 Intermediate Archery focuses on intermediate archery safety, form, and introduces new equipment, styles, including Recurve and Comound bows. Fun games and form reinforcement techniques are emphasized in this class.

Equipment provided with exception of safety kit.

Cost: $120/month

Pre-reqs:

  • A101 or recommendation from private instructor
  • 7 years and older

Advanced

A103 Advanced Archery focuses on a continued education toward tournament styles of shooting used by many archery tournament associations around the world. Participants will also gain knowledge around shooting form and advice on personal equipment selections.

Equipment will be provided with exception of safety kit.

Cost: $120/month

Pre-reqs:

  • A101 and A102 or equivalent experience
  • 7 years and older

Private lessons

By appointment

Straight to the Point facilities are open to athletes for practice time during business hours, but lanes are available on a first come-first served basis and at a cost of $15/hour. Athletes below A103 or equivalent experience must have an instructor present during open practice time. Children age 16 and younger must have a parent or guardian present.
